About Us

{#
#} {# Introduction#} {#
#}

MetaBanks is a financial technology company specializing in delivering state-of-the-art banking solutions. The team at MetaBanks consists of highly experienced personnel with extensive banking and technology backgrounds. MetaBanks works hand-in-hand with clients providing them with top notch IT solutions across a wide spectrum of fundamental banking activities including but not limited to retail banking, corporate banking, investment banking, group regulatory compliance and risk management to streamline day-to-day workload in an efficient manner while enabling decision makers with the technology and real time intelligence needed to take instant decisions with confidence.

MetaBanks differentiates itself in the marketplace by taking a bottom-up approach to product development by abstracting the complexity of the core banking through the MetaBanks Middleware that allows for out-of-the-box integration and rapid deployment of its solutions within the clients perimeter.

Mission

Our mission is to ensure the highest standards of quality in delivering state-of-the-art technology solutions for the banking industry.

{# LEARN MORE#}

Goal

Our goal is to revolutionize the technical integration process of banking solutions with the core banking system through the use of the MetaBanks Middleware, enabling our clients to adopt new banking solutions through a plug-and-play approach, extensively reducing the overall integration time and eliminating associated risks.

{# LEARN MORE#}

Vision

Our vision is to advance the banking sector in the Middle East and North Africa by bridging local banks with global banking solution providers, allowing banks to adopt cutting edge solutions irrespective of the underlying core banking system in place.

Products

#} {# Our Solutions#} {#
#}

Core Banking Middleware

The MetaBanks Middleware is at the heart of all other MetaBanks solutions, it introduces an architectural layer on top of the Core Banking system in order to abstract the underlying complexity of the Core Banking business logic.


Learn More

Web/Mobile Online Banking

MetaBanks Web/Mobile Banking solution provides end consumers with an enhanced highly secured online banking experience, improving service offerings and allowing customers to easily access their accounts, perform transactions, carry out day-to-day payments, check balances and issue account statements on demand.


Learn More


Exposure Management System (EMS)

MetaBanks Exposure Management System (EMS) delivers real-time Business Intelligence, Analytics and Reporting tools that provide an instant view of current exposure standing with other bank correspondents. The system is based on providing real time exposure data correlated against Limits for actual Trade Finance and Treasury transactions ...


Learn More

Approval Management System

The MetaBanks Approval Management System (AMS) is a dynamic workflow engine designed to facilitate and automate the approvals process required for various banking activities. The system allows for various workflows to be setup and configured, the automated service extracts essential information needed for the approval process ...
