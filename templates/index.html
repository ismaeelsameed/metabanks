About Us

Introduction

MetaBanks is a financial technology company specializing in delivering state-of-the-art banking solutions. The team at MetaBanks consists of highly experienced personnel with extensive banking and technology backgrounds. MetaBanks works hand-in-hand with clients providing them with top notch IT solutions across a wide spectrum of fundamental banking activities including but not limited to retail banking, corporate banking, investment banking, group regulatory compliance and risk management to streamline day-to-day workload in an efficient manner while enabling decision makers with the technology and real time intelligence needed to take instant decisions with confidence.

MetaBanks differentiates itself in the marketplace by taking a bottom-up approach to product development by abstracting the complexity of the core banking through the MetaBanks Middleware that allows for out-of-the-box integration and rapid deployment of its solutions within the clients perimeter.
