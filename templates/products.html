Exposure Management System

Products

MetaBanks Exposure Management System (EMS) delivers real-time Business Intelligence, Analytics and Reporting tools that provide an instant view of current exposure standing with other bank correspondents. The system is based on providing real time exposure data correlated against Limits for actual Trade Finance and Treasury transactions that have been conducted. EMS provides Executive Management with accurate and real time intelligence that allows for instant decision making in times of crisis and eases day-to-day decision making for the Financial Institutions department. EMS also provides pro-active control flags including reminders & alarms notifying of breaches in limits.
