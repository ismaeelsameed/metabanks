Exposure Management System

Products

MetaBanks Exposure Management System (EMS) delivers real-time Business Intelligence, Analytics and Reporting tools that provide an instant view of current exposure standing with other bank correspondents. The system is based on providing real time exposure data correlated against Limits for actual Trade Finance and Treasury transactions that have been conducted. EMS provides Executive Management with accurate and real time intelligence that allows for instant decision making in times of crisis and eases day-to-day decision making for the Financial Institutions department. EMS also provides pro-active control flags including reminders & alarms notifying of breaches in limits. A comprehensive list of the EMS's features include:

  • Limits Reports: Provide a unique way for FIs to monitor Limits granted for each Bank/Country based on limit type.
  • Exposure Reports: monitoring Trade Finance and Treasury exposures by Bank/Country.
  • Limits vs. Exposure: monitoring Limits against exposures and setting Pro-active control flags which will notify users of breaches in limits.
  • Portfolio Reports: Managing FIs portfolio as a whole in terms of Total exposure, Incoming/Outgoing business, Funded vs unfunded exposure, Beneficiaries and Applicants, Top 20 exposures and Treasury exposure.
  • Profitability Reports: monitoring FI profitability by a time frame and providing profitability dashboards which will help in maximizing profits that the FI Department generates.
  • Relationship Reports: monitoring Business Direction (Incoming/Outgoing/RMA) to enhance the relationship with FI’s counterparty.
  • Workflow: Workflow is designed to facilitate and automate the approvals process presented to the Credit Committee for FI transactions & credit review processing. The automated transaction will extract all available information related to the counterparty under review from the system, creating an efficient way to process transactions and reducing human error.
