Web/Mobile Online Banking

Products

MetaBanks Web/Mobile Banking solution provides end consumers with an enhanced highly secured online banking experience, improving service offerings and allowing customers to easily access their accounts, perform transactions, carry out day-to-day payments, check balances and issue account statements on demand. A comprehensive list of features of the MetaBanks Web/Mobile banking solution includes:

  • Check account balances and details for all accounts including current, savings and credit cards.
  • Generate Account Statements instantly from the web or mobile application.
  • View and search transactions on various filters such as amount, date etc..
  • Initiate the application process for opening new accounts such as current, savings and credit cards.
  • Initiate the application process for requesting new loans.
  • Set up and schedule transfers and bill payments.
  • Perform instant intrabank transfers.
  • Deposit cheques from the mobile application by capturing the front and back of the cheque.
  • Search ATMs or branches the users current location.
  • Perform currency conversion on the spot.
